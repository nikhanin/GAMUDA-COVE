Paya Indah Discovery Wetlands @ Gamuda Cove


Kuala Langat, Selangor

Our Story


Paya Indah Discovery Wetlands is the Naature Park of Gamuda Cove's attractions, with all its existing activities curated from the wetlands' available and existing natural landscape. Whether fishing, camping or sightseeing in nature, visitors can expect a rejuvenating and stimulating adventure here at Paya Indah Discovery Wetlands. Experience a fulfilling nature-centric educational program at the all-new Wetlands Arboretum Centre. All GL Play tickets are available for walk-in purchase at the GL Play Ticket Hub @ The Hive, Discovery Park.
