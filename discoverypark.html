About Us


Discovery Park @ Gamuda Cove, which opened its doors to public in January 2020, is the gateway to the Paya Indah Discovery Wetlands, a national wildlife sanctuary wetland gazetted area. Set amid real nature, this 23-acre Sporting Adventure park offers an exciting and unique experience perfect for team building and adventure seekers. As the Adventure Park of Gamuda Cove, Discovery Park's attractions are inspired by the idea to bring a sense of vibrancy, grit and thrill that will Create Memorable Experiences for visitors. Looking for the ultimate sports & team building destination? Your search ends at Discovery Park. All GL Play tickets are available for walk-in purchase at the GL Play Ticket Hub @ The Hive, Discovery Park.
